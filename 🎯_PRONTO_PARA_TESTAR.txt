   - Clique "Nova Questão"
   - Preencha formulário
   - Clique "Salvar"
   - Network: POST /api/colaborador/questoes
   - Status: 201 Created ✅

   - Confirme
   - Network: DELETE /api/colaborador/questoes/{id}
   - Status: 204 No Content ✅
